A Going Concern

I was approached by author Brian Railsback who was looking for someone to handle the layout of his slightly-less-grim than most post-apocalyptic novel, A Going Concern. Along with the interior layout and cover illustration I helped him setup distribution through Amazon's print-on-demand and Kindle platforms, giving him the keys to a fully set-up publishing avenue at the end of the project.
